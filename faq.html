Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit on the size of the files that I can upload?

Files uploaded to Slashtmp from our website are restricted to 100MB each but the good news is you can upload as many files as you like. We're planning on increasing the individual file limits to 500MB each very soon.

How long will you store my files before they expire?

Currently, Slashtmp has a hard limit of 24 hours for all file uploads. This means that after 24 hours of the upload your files will be permanently deleted from our system. It is also possible (although highly unlikely) that your files may be lost due to an unexpected system failure. You should not be using Slashtmp for backups.

Can others access my files without my permission?

Files uploaded to Slashtmp are publically accessible. To access a file a user needs to know its system identifier. These identifiers are randomly generated and difficult to guess, as such it would is difficult (but not impossible) for someone to come across your file but randomly guessing its identifier.

How much does Slashtmp cost?

Slashtmp is completely free! Both unregistered and (coming soon) registered users get unlimited file storage at no charge. We are contemplating premium features that may incur costs but our free plan will continue to be as generous as it is today, if not more so.
