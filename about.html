About Us

Slashtmp.io is a technology company building delightfully simple, easy to use tools for sharing any type of files from any Internet enabled device. We aim to make the process of sharing your files with others simple and enjoyable. As such, you can begin and continue using our products without any account registration and still enjoy full, unrestricted access to our primary service.

Why are we doing this?

There are many services on the Internet that provide cloud storage already, and they are awesome. Sometimes though you just want to share something quick with a friend or a colleague, or you need a temporary place to stash your files. This is where we come in. Our service provides a quick, barrier free access to unlimited storage for free.

Our platform is designed to provide you with what you need to get the job done. No more, no less!

Our team is dedicated to providing technology solutions and tools that are focused and useful. We are committed to this goal and aim to continue to expand and improve this service to give you more of what you need but at the same time maintaining its simplicity and flexibility.

What's next?

At the moment Slashtmp is in Beta. It's free now and it will continue to be so. We thank you for visiting our site and hope that you will give it a try.
